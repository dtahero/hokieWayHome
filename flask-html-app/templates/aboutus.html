About the Hokies Way Home Team

We are a team of Virginia Tech students dedicated to using technology to support our fellow Hokies. In a day and a half, we developed a website designed specifically for Virginia Tech students looking for off-campus housing. With MongoDB for data management and Python with Flask for backend services, we efficiently split our tasks between backend and frontend development. Despite facing challenges with integrating these components, we successfully connected them through careful debugging and collaboration. We used GitHub for version control and Visual Studio Code’s Live Share feature to collaborate in real-time. Our commitment to helping our classmates is at the heart of our project, and we’re excited to add features like favorites and login to enhance the user experience. Our goal is to make finding the perfect apartment a seamless and enjoyable process for all Hokies.
